Attendance management system
1. User login (Student/Intern/Office staff)
2. Small profile of each entry
3. In and out time for office staff and intern and In for students.
4. Everyone must first signup
5. After login on locally hosted server one must login.
6. After login one must see a small profile including information
   along with current date and time.
7.Interns and office staff must have option for in and out button
  which stores current date and time.
8. Students should have option to select in button and time must be stored.
9.Profile should include course enrolled for students, intern topic for intern,
  and small bio for all other added information such as email, phone address.
10. admin should be able to check the total attendance. 
11. late arrival, absent etc should be noted.
